FAQs on Free Domains

Find answers to frequently asked questions about free domains.

What is a domain name?

A domain name is a website’s address. All websites that you can access online have one. With domains, you can seamlessly navigate different websites. Alternatively, you can access websites by using their IP addresses. However, since they contain a string of numbers, they are more difficult to remember than domain names.

Domain extensions can be generic (.com, .net, .online) or specific to a location or purpose of the site (.store, .in, .edu).

Is a list of free domain name extensions available?

The domain extensions available for free include: .co.in, .org.in, .xyz, .net, .in, .shop, .online, .com, .info, .tech, .site, .store, .website, .space, .icu, .uno, .fun, .pw, .host, .link, .click, .help, .me, .press.

Unlike less popular TLDs, all these 100% free domain extensions are more trustworthy. Claim your domain extension or top-level domain before it is no longer available.

Security is our top priority as a web hosting company. Therefore, we will also give you an SSL certificate for free, making your site run on HTTPS securely.
